# The last memory

## **Project title and short description**

**The Last Memory** is an atmospheric VR exploration experience where the player moves through a surreal world made of memories.

Instead of completing traditional tasks, the player interacts with objects to reveal fragments of past moments, experience their emotions, and let them fade away.

The game focuses on emotional immersion, minimal guidance, and intuitive interaction, allowing the player to feel the process of remembering and letting go.

## **Idea and Goal of the Project**

**Idea:**

The project explores the concept of memory as a living, interactive space. The player takes the role of a memory-like entity, moving through fragments of a life and interacting with them through touch and presence.

**Goal:**

- To create an emotional VR experience without explicit storytelling
- To convey feelings such as nostalgia, warmth, loneliness, and acceptance through interaction
- To use VR as a tool for immersion and personal reflection
- To design a non-linear experience where meaning is felt rather than explained
- To represent the process of remembering and letting go of memories

## Steps Completed So Far

- Developed the core concept and narrative direction of the project
- Designed the main interaction mechanic (touch → hold → release)
- Created two playable environments:
    - Childhood bedroom (representing early memories and comfort)
    - Kitchen (representing family and everyday life)
    - 2 pickable objects (teddy and childhood picture)
- Implemented basic VR interaction with objects
- Planned additional environment (ruins) and puzzle-based interactions
